Together In Lebanon

Gathering Rotarians from around the world in Lebanon

Beirut, October 5 - 11, 2015

View Schedule

  • Discover Lebanon

    Experience Lebanon by immersing yourself in its rich cultural and religious heritage, mingling with the Lebanese and exploring various regions.

  • Network

    Benefit from professional networking sessions with fellow Rotarians, visit projects completed in Lebanon and meet locally twinned clubs.

  • Foster Friendships

    The goal of Together in Lebanon is to strengthen the bond between local Rotarians, their friends and the larger Rotarian community!

Why Together in Lebanon?

The purpose of the meeting is to enable Rotarians worldwide to discover (or rediscover) Lebanon today, and to create new friendship ties with their Lebanese peer. The agenda for this meeting includes Rotarian and touristic activities. United by the love of Lebanon, what better time and setting for Rotarians and friends to meet and share old experiences while creating new ones! Joining us will be:

  • Rotarian friends of Lebanon
  • Rotarians of clubs twinned with Lebanese clubs
  • Rotarians of Lebanese origin, along with their families and friends.
  • Rotarians from Districts and Countries members of an ICC with Lebanon
  • Rotarian members of Fellowship and Action Groups that have members in the Lebanese Rotary Community.
